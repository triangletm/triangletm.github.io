About Us

Toastmasters is a worldwide non-profit organization dedicated to helping individuals build communication and leadership skills.

Serving Northern Kentucky and Greater Cincinnati since 1953, Triangle Toastmasters is a friendly local club where you can practice and improve your skills alongside a supportive and diverse group.


Whether you're a student, professional, or just looking to try something new, Toastmasters is the perfect starting point!

Contact Us

Visitors are always welcome to any meeting. Feel free to drop in to one of our meetings, or contact us about scheduling a visit.
